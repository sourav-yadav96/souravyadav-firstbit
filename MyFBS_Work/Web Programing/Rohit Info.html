Rohit Sharma - The Hitman of Indian Cricket

Rohit Gurunath Sharma is one of India's most celebrated cricketers. Known for his elegant batting style and explosive performances, he is regarded as one of the best limited-overs batsmen in the world.

Career Highlights

  • Born: April 30, 1987
  • ODI Debut: June 2007 vs Ireland
  • Test Debut: November 2013 vs West Indies
  • Captain: Current captain of the Indian national team (as of 2024)

Records & Achievements

Rohit Sharma holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs: 264 runs against Sri Lanka in 2014.

He is the only player to score 3 double centuries in One Day Internationals.

In the 2019 ICC World Cup, he scored 5 centuries — the most by any player in a single edition.

Nickname

Fans fondly call him "Hitman" because of his ability to hit big sixes with ease.

Personal Life

Rohit was born in Nagpur, Maharashtra. He married Ritika Sajdeh in 2015, and they have a daughter named Samaira.
